Rohit Sharma

Indian Cricketer

Rohit Gurunath Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats.
He is a right-handed batsman and occasional right-arm off-break bowler. Sharma is regarded as one of the best opening batsmen in limited-overs cricket.
